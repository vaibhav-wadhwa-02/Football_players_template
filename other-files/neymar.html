About Neymar Jr.

Neymar da Silva Santos Junior, commonly known as Neymar Jr., was born on February 5, 1992, in Mogi das Cruzes, Brazil. He is a professional football player who currently plays as a forward for Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team.

Neymar began playing football at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks. At the age of 11, he was offered a spot in the youth academy of Santos FC, one of Brazil's most prestigious football clubs. He made his professional debut for the club at the age of 17, and quickly became one of the team's star players.

In 2013, Neymar made a high-profile transfer to Barcelona, where he formed a lethal attacking trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez. During his time at the club, he helped lead Barcelona to numerous victories, including two La Liga titles and the UEFA Champions League in 2015. He also won the Ballon d'Or award for the best player in the world in 2015.
