About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, is widely regarded as one of the greatest soccer players of all time. He began his soccer career at a young age, joining Andorinha when he was just eight years old. He later played for Nacional and Sporting Lisbon, where his impressive skills caught the attention of scouts from around Europe.

In 2003, Ronaldo signed with Manchester United, where he quickly established himself as a star player. He helped lead the team to numerous victories, including three Premier League titles and a Champions League title.
