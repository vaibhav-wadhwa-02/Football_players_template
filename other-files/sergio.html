Sergio Ramos - Biography

Sergio Ramos Garcia was born on March 30, 1986, in Camas, Seville, Spain. He is a professional football player who currently plays as a center-back for Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) and the Spain national team. Ramos is widely considered one of the best defenders of his generation, known for his aggressive style of play and leadership on the field.

Ramos began his football career at a young age, playing for Sevilla's youth team before making his first-team debut in 2004. He quickly established himself as a star player and helped lead the team to victory in the UEFA Cup in 2006. In 2005, he was signed by Real Madrid, where he has played ever since. He has since won numerous titles with the team, including five La Liga titles, four Champions League titles, and four Club World Cup titles.

Ramos is also known for his success with the Spain national team, having won the UEFA European Championship in 2008 and 2012, as well as the FIFA World Cup in 2010. He has earned over 180 caps for Spain, making him the country's most-capped player of all time.

In addition to his success on the field, Ramos is also known for his charity work. He has been involved in various initiatives to help underprivileged children, including the Sergio Ramos Foundation, which aims to promote the education and integration of children and young people through sports.

Ramos is married to television presenter Pilar Rubio, and the couple has four children together. He is also known for his tattoos, which cover much of his arms and legs.

Overall, Sergio Ramos is widely considered one of the best defenders of his generation, known for his skill, leadership, and passion on the field. His success with both Real Madrid and the Spain national team has cemented his place as one of the greatest football players of all time.
