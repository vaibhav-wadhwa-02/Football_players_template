About Bruno Fernandes

Bruno Fernandes, born on September 8, 1994, in Maia, Portugal, is a professional football player who currently plays as a midfielder for Manchester United and the Portugal national team. He began his football career playing for local club Boavista before joining Italian team Novara in 2012.

After impressing with his performances, Bruno signed with Udinese in 2013, where he spent two seasons before joining Sampdoria. His performances for Sampdoria earned him a move to Sporting CP in Portugal, where he quickly established himself as one of the best players in the league.

Off the field, Bruno is known for his humility and work ethic. He has spoken openly about the sacrifices he has made to get to where he is today and the importance of hard work and dedication. He is also involved in various charitable causes, including the fight against childhood cancer.
